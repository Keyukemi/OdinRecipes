Jollof Rice

Description

Jollof, or jollof rice, is a rice dish from West Africa. The dish is typically made with long-grain rice, tomatoes, onions, spices, vegetables and meat in a single pot, although its ingredients and preparation methods vary across different regions.


Ingredients

  • 2 cups of White Rice
  • 2 Scoops of Tomato paste
  • 3 Spoons of Tomato and pepper Puree
  • One bulb of medium sized Onions
  • A bowl of Beef stock
  • A cup of Groundnut Oil
  • Seasonings i.e. Salt, MSG, Curry and Thyme
  • Raw Ginger and Garlic (or powder versions)

Steps

  1. Heat up the Groundnut oil in low heat
  2. Add your Sliced Onions
  3. Add your tomato paste and fry till it forms tiny clumps
  4. Add your tomato and pepper puree
  5. Add your Seasonings to taste and let the mix simmer for another 5 minutes
  6. Add your Beef Stock
  7. Add two cups of water and allow the mixture boil
  8. Add your washed rice
  9. Let the rice cook till its soft.
  10. Serve with Meat of choice
