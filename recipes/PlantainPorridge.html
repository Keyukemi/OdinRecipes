Plantain Porridge

Description

Nigerian Plantain Porridge is a rich meal that all plantain lovers will surely enjoy. It can be prepared with either ripe or unripe plantains or a combination of both.


Even if youâ€™re not a plantain lover, you should give this meal a try, because plantains are rich in fiber, minerals(potasium, magnesium, iron, phosphorous) and Vitamins(A, B6 and C)

Ingredients

  • 3 large sized unripe plantains
  • 5 Pieces of Roasted Hake fish
  • 3 Tablespoons of pepper Puree
  • One bulb of medium sized Onions
  • A cup of Palm Oil
  • A bowl of fresh vegetables(Ugwu)
  • Seasonings i.e. Salt, MSG, Curry and Thyme

Steps

  1. Peel your unripe Plantain, and cut to medium sized
  2. Add your Sliced Onions
  3. Add your pepper puree
  4. Let the Plantain cook till its soft.
  5. Add your roasted hake fish
  6. Add your Seasonings to taste and let the mix simmer for another 5 minutes
  7. Add your Palm oil and let its simmer for another 5 minutes
  8. Add your washed vegetables and stir into the mix
  9. Let it boil on low heat for 10 min
  10. Serve with garnishing of choice
