Asaro (Yam Porridge)

Description

Asaro which is also known as Yam pottage or Yam porridge is a very delicious Yam recipe though it’s eaten by most tribes in Nigeria however, it is more common in the Yoruba speaking parts of Nigeria. It’s also easy to make. If you love yam, it’s a must-try recipe!


Ingredients

  • One tuber of yam
  • 5 Pieces of Roasted Hake fish
  • 3 Tablespoons of pepper Puree
  • One bulb of medium sized Onions
  • A cup of Palm Oil
  • A bowl of fresh vegetables(Ugwu)
  • Seasonings i.e. Salt, MSG, Curry and Thyme

Steps

  1. Peel your yam, and cut to medium sized
  2. Add your Sliced Onions
  3. Add your pepper puree
  4. Let the yam cook till its soft.
  5. Add your roasted hake fish
  6. Add your Seasonings to taste and let the mix simmer for another 5 minutes
  7. Add your Palm oil and let its simmer for another 5 minutes
  8. Add your washed vegetables and stir into the mix
  9. Let it boil on low heat for 10 min
  10. Serve with garnishing of choice
